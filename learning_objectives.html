My learning objectives with Simba Technology Limited

Based on the activities I engaged in during my internship at Simba Technology in the Banking and Cash Deposit Machines Department, here are some learning objectives I have achieved:

1. Proficiency in SQL Implementation: Through learning and implementing SQL, you gained practical experience in working with databases, writing queries, and manipulating data, making you proficient in SQL.

2. Technical Skills in Cash Deposit Machines Maintenance: Your involvement in servicing, maintaining, and cleaning cash deposit machines provided you with technical skills in diagnosing and resolving hardware issues, ensuring the smooth functioning of these machines.

3. Problem-Solving and Troubleshooting Abilities: By fixing issues in token machines, such as conflicting primary and foreign keys, you developed problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot complex technical problems.

4. Practical Application of Database Management Concepts: Your experiences in handling token machine issues and learning about the impact of primary and foreign keys on system performance allowed you to apply theoretical database management concepts in real-world scenarios.

5. Effective Communication with Clients and Teams: Interacting with various companies like Lukka Plastics, City Tyres, and Centenary Bank to provide support and maintenance for their machines enhanced your communication skills, both with clients and internal teams.

6. Project Management and Prioritization: Balancing multiple tasks like machine servicing, database issue resolution, and maintenance required you to manage your time effectively and prioritize tasks to meet project deadlines.

7. Understanding of Banking Operations: Working on projects related to cash deposit machines in the banking sector exposed you to the intricacies of banking operations and the importance of efficiency and accuracy in financial transactions.

8. Database Optimization and Performance Improvement: Your efforts in resolving token machine issues taught you how to optimize database performance, ensuring faster response times and improved user experiences.

9. Continuous Learning and Adaptability: Dealing with different machines, clients, and technical challenges demanded adaptability and continuous learning, allowing you to stay up-to-date with industry advancements.

10. Team Collaboration and Cooperation: Collaborating with technical teams, clients, and colleagues at Simba Technology and other companies honed your teamwork and collaboration skills, contributing to successful project outcomes.

11. Customer-Centric Approach: Providing support and maintenance services to clients required you to adopt a customer-centric approach, ensuring client satisfaction and building positive relationships.

12. Professional Growth and Confidence: Successfully handling tasks related to databases, machines, and client interactions would have boosted your professional growth and confidence in your technical abilities.

These learning objectives demonstrate the diverse set of skills and knowledge I gained during my internship, making it a valuable and enriching experience for my personal and professional development.
