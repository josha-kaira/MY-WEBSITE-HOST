4. Flexible Development Approach: Adopting an agile development approach to accommodate client-specific requirements and gather feedback throughout the development process.

5. Effective Time Management: Using time management techniques, such as setting priorities and breaking down tasks, to manage multiple projects effectively.

6. Hardware Compatibility Testing: Conducting thorough testing and simulations to ensure hardware compatibility and functionality for the new cash machine prototype.

7. Data Encryption and Access Controls: Implementing robust data encryption and access control measures to protect sensitive information and comply with data privacy regulations.

8. Thorough Presentation Preparation: Preparing comprehensive sales presentations with clear communication and addressing potential client concerns in advance.

9. Database Migration Planning: Thoroughly planning the database migration process, conducting extensive testing, and having backup measures in place to mitigate potential issues.

10. Comprehensive User Training: Providing comprehensive user training, documentation, and ongoing support to ensure successful adoption of new systems by clients and users.
