Role and Responsibilities

As an intern at Simba Technology, my responsibilities included

1. SQL Database Management: - Assisting in SQL database management tasks, including data entry, data manipulation, and query optimization. - Implementing SQL queries to retrieve and update data in the token machine and cash deposit machine databases. - Participating in database performance optimization efforts to ensure efficient data handling.

2. Cash Deposit Machine Servicing: - Conducting routine servicing and maintenance of cash deposit machines at client locations, such as Lukka Plastics and City Tyres. - Cleaning and maintaining the internal components of the cash deposit machines to ensure optimal functionality. - Troubleshooting hardware issues and providing timely resolution to minimize machine downtime.

3. Token Machine Issue Resolution: - Investigating and resolving token machine issues reported by clients, for example, identifying and fixing conflicting primary and foreign keys that affected system performance. - Working closely with the development team to implement solutions to enhance the token machine system's efficiency and reliability.

4. On-Site Support: - Providing on-site support to clients, including Centenary Bank, to troubleshoot and fix token machine and cash deposit machine issues. - Collaborating with technical teams to diagnose problems and ensure prompt resolution to maintain smooth operations for clients.

5. Learning and Implementing SQL: - Actively participating in SQL learning sessions to gain knowledge about database management, queries, and optimizations. - Implementing SQL concepts learned during training in real-world scenarios to enhance the token machine and cash deposit machine databases.

6. Documentation: - Documenting the servicing and maintenance activities performed on cash deposit machines at different client locations. - Creating reports on resolved token machine issues, including steps taken for troubleshooting and solutions implemented.

7. Collaboration with Development Team: - Collaborating with the development team to provide feedback on token machine system performance and user experience, contributing to product improvement.

8. Client Training: - Assisting in conducting training sessions for clients, such as Lukka Plastics and City Tyres, on the usage and maintenance of cash deposit machines.

9. Quality Assurance: - Participating in quality assurance activities to ensure that cash deposit machines and token machines meet quality standards and perform optimally.

10. Continuous Learning: - Proactively seeking opportunities to enhance skills and knowledge in database management, token machine technology, and related areas.

Those are just some of the roles and responsibilities I have been engaged in during your internship at Simba Technology. My contributions in these areas have contributed to the smooth functioning and improvement of token machines and cash deposit machines for various clients.
